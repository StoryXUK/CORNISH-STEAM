About

As a collaborative platform across the STEAM fields (Science, Technology, Engineering, Art, and Mathematics). We believe that business coaching for inclusivity and diversity is essential in creating more sustainable futures. Here's why:

Challenges in the STEAM Industries: The STEAM fields face significant issues related to inclusivity and diversity. Women, underrepresented minorities, and marginalised groups often encounter barriers to social mobility and advancement. This lack of representation limits innovation, hinders diverse perspectives, and impedes progress in these industries.

How Coaching Can Help

Fostering Inclusive Workplaces: Our coaching guides organisations in developing strategies to foster diversity and inclusion. By promoting equal opportunities, fair treatment, and representation, we create inclusive workplaces that value and respect individuals from all backgrounds.

Advancing Social Mobility: Through coaching, we support individuals from underrepresented groups in developing the necessary skills, confidence, and networks to overcome barriers and advance their careers. We provide guidance to unlock their potential and access opportunities for social mobility.

Driving Innovation and Collaboration: Diversity in the STEAM fields fuels innovation and problem-solving. Our coaching helps teams leverage diverse perspectives, experiences, and backgrounds to drive breakthroughs and collaborative solutions. By embracing inclusivity, organisations unlock their full innovative potential.

Expanding Access and Representation: We guide organisations in cultivating inclusive cultures that attract diverse talent. Our coaching helps organisations overcome biases in recruitment and retention, ensuring that underrepresented groups have equal access to opportunities. This expands representation and creates a more inclusive environment.

Supporting Ethical and Responsible Practices: Inclusivity and diversity are central to ethical and responsible business practices. Our coaching equips organisations with the tools and knowledge to navigate complex ethical challenges. We promote fair treatment, equal opportunities, and non-discriminatory practices for a more sustainable future.

Get in touch
Join us at CORNISH STEAM and be part of the movement to empower tomorrow through business coaching for sustainable futures. Contact us now to learn more and take the first step towards a brighter, more dynamic future in the STEAM industries.
- Serena Rosaria, Founder
